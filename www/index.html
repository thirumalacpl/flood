DECEMBER 3


Firstly, please forgive me If my team of volunteers or myself haven't been able to rescue or reach relief supplies to you or the ones who you had personally requested. It was a very hectic day of service with very poor network facilities. 13220 food packets were distributed today by the service minded and passionate youth volunteers at the Agni Foundations. 184 families have been evacuated across 40 different locations. Credits to several donors who provided the meals. The rains have subsided for now, but the situation is very criticial in Kotturpuram, alwarpet, thambaram, nandhanam, velachery, saidapet, Anna Nagar.Many areas are bad but improving. Several lakes and rivers are overflowing. The water is flowing back into the streets as the the drain system are clogged. 250 Foundations volunteers and myself have been working tirelessly day and night to help those who are severly affected. We are not only providing food but also places to stay across chennai. Many of the places are inaccessible and dangerous. We haven't been able to rescue all those who requested but we are still trying our best. Tommorow we will be renting a couple of boats to reach relief supplies to the affected. Hopefully, that will enable us to optimise our operations. Network has been down and internet is very hard to access. Many roads are inaccessible and dangerous. We haven't been able to rescue all those who requested but we are still trying our best. Right now I would sincerely request all of you to stay indoors. Please stock rations and use water very conservatively. Please help us connect with the national news agencies such as Times Now, NDTV and CNN-IBN to help streamline our rescue and relief operations in the coming days. We have volunteers across the city who can not only offer food but also a place to stay.
